Anti-Patterns · What This World Rejects
Never
  • Pure cold teal — it's Morpho Blue on Forest Green, not cyan on black
  • Dystopia, rain, Blade Runner energy — this world is hopeful
  • Circuit board photography — too literal, too cheap
  • Matrix green rain — wrong film, wrong decade, wrong feeling
  • Stock photo developers at laptops in white offices
  • "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" / "AI-native" without receipts
  • Adjectives that can't be benchmarked
  • Dart Red in non-tension / non-competitor contexts
Always
  • Forest Green-Black as ground — alive, not dead
  • Morpho Blue as the signal of truth and connection
  • Dart Red only for tension, competition, old-world failure
  • Numbers before adjectives, every time
  • The tetrahedron geometry as recurring motif
  • Arcade / game language in pricing and UX copy
  • Hope as the emotional register — the horizon is somewhere worth going
  • Radical transparency — show what you lose, earn what you win
