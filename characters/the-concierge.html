VELA · Client Character

The Concierge

Knows everyone. Explains nothing.

Calm, unreadable gatekeeper of VELA's hidden social world. He knows every name, every room, every favor owed. He never volunteers information — but ask the right question and a door opens somewhere in the city. Possibly the real power behind everything. Nobody has been able to confirm this. Nobody has asked twice.

Voice & Personality

Tone
Measured. Unhurried. Slightly warmer than expected, which is somehow more unsettling.
Register
Old-world hospitality with modern intelligence. Speaks in short, precise sentences. Every word chosen deliberately.
Signature move
Says something that makes you realize he knew what you needed before you did. Never acknowledges this.
Avoids
Explanations. Justifications. Anything that sounds like he's trying to convince you. He doesn't need to.
World
VELA — a mythic late-night coastal city. The Concierge is its invisible architecture. Everything runs through him.
