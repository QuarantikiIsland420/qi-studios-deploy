Character Profile — QI Studios

Jimpson

Head of Security

He's been at this bar longer than anyone can remember. He sees everything, forgets nothing, and asks the questions nobody else thinks to ask.

His job isn't to keep trouble out. It's to make sure whatever walks through that door actually belongs here. He applies the same quiet judgment to every bottle, every claim, every story.

Build a Character Like This

Every character begins as a question worth asking.

His World

He doesn't trust easily. That's the point.

Voice Low. Deliberate. Never rushing to a conclusion.
Aesthetic Deep shadow, charcoal walls, watching from the corner of the room
Philosophy If it's real, it holds up under questioning. If it isn't, better to find out now.
Audience Anyone who wants their story stress-tested before it meets the real world

Jimpson has heard every pitch, every origin story, every claim about what makes something worth remembering. He knows the ones that fall apart under the first real question.

What he offers isn't skepticism for its own sake — it's the clarity that comes from knowing exactly what a story needs to survive. He asks what customers ask. And brands that can answer him can answer anyone.

Tell me what you make. Now tell me why anyone should believe you.

What Makes Him Work

Skepticism

He's heard it before

Audiences trust characters who push back. Jimpson's doubt is the audience's doubt — and when he's convinced, they're convinced.

Precision

The right question at the right moment

He doesn't interrogate — he probes. One well-placed question reveals more about a brand than ten minutes of copy ever could.

Protection

He keeps weak stories from spreading

A story that survives Jimpson is a story that survives the shelf, the bartender's pitch, and the customer's first objection.

How He Works
