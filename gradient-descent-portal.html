About the Author

"What I didn't know, for a long time, was whether I was minimizing the right loss function."

A. Sarapultseva was born in Obninsk — a Soviet science city built for physicists, where mathematics was simply how most people thought. Psychology and neuroscience at Moscow State University. A decade in Silicon Valley optimizing things for a living. A sabbatical disguised as collapse: yoga, India, travel, Costa Rica. The book arrived during a morning meditation in a humid garden. She lives in Costa Rica with her son. She is still descending.
